About Me




I love to build. From K'Nex ball towers and Lego structures when I was younger to websites and iPhone apps now, the one constant in my life has been my desire to bring my ideas to life.


I graduated from Los Altos High School and will now major in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) at UC Berkeley. I will also be pursuing a certificate in Technology Entrepreneurship.


In my free time, I enjoy hanging out with friends, playing ultimate frisbee, and of course, creating products for the world to use!
